ជំពូកទី១៖ គណិតវិទ្យា​លើក​ទី​២ (Calculus II)

  • ១.១ Integration Techniques – Integration by Parts, Trigonometric Substitution, Partial Fractions
  • ១.២ Improper Integrals & Convergence Tests
  • ១.៣ Applications of Integration – Volumes, Arc Length, Surface Area
  • ១.៤ Differential Equations – First‑order linear, separable, exact equations
  • ១.៥ Series & Sequences – Power Series, Taylor & Maclaurin Series, Convergence

ជំពូកទី២៖ អាល់ហ្ស៊ីម (Linear Algebra)

  • ២.១ Matrices – Operations, Inverses, Determinants
  • ២.២ Systems of Linear Equations – Gaussian Elimination, Cramer’s Rule
  • ២.៣ Vector Spaces – Basis, Dimension, Rank‑Nullity
  • ២.៤ Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors – Diagonalisation, Applications
  • ២.៥ Linear Transformations – Matrices representation, Change of basis

ជំពូកទី៣៖ ត្រីកោណមាត្រ​កម្រិត​ខ្ពស់ (Advanced Trigonometry & Analytic Geometry)

  • ៣.១ Polar Coordinates & Graphs
  • ៣.២ Conic Sections – Parabola, Ellipse, Hyperbola (focus‑directrix form)
  • ៣.៣ Vectors in the Plane – Dot product, cross product (2‑D), projection
  • ៣.៤ Rotation & Reflection Matrices
  • ៣.៥ 3‑D Geometry – Equations of planes, lines, distance formulas

ជំពូកទី៤៖ ស្ថិតិ និង​ប្រមាណវិធី​ខ្ពស់ (Advanced Statistics & Probability)

  • ៤.១ Random Variables – Discrete & Continuous distributions
  • ៤.២ Expected Value, Variance, Standard Deviation, Moments
  • ៤.៣ Common Distributions – Normal, Binomial, Poisson, Exponential
  • ៤.৪ Hypothesis Testing – t‑test, chi‑square, ANOVA basics
  • ៤.⑤ Regression & Correlation – Linear regression, coefficient of determination

ជំពូកទី៥៖ បញ្ហា​ឧបករណ៍​ (Applied Problem Solving)

  • ៥.១ Modelling with Differential Equations (Population, Motion, RC circuits)
  • ៥.២ Optimisation – Lagrange multipliers (introductory)
  • ៥.៣ Financial Mathematics – Interest, annuities, amortisation
  • ៥.៤ Mathematical Induction & Proof Techniques
  • ៥.៥ Project‑Based Assignment – Combine several topics in one real‑world case study
